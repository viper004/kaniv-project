About Us

Who We Are

Hearts United is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive change in communities worldwide. Founded in 2010, we've grown from a small local initiative to an international charity with projects in over 20 countries.

Our mission is to address the root causes of poverty and inequality through sustainable solutions that empower individuals and transform communities.

Global Reach

Active in 20+ countries with 50 ongoing projects

Transparent

90% of donations go directly to program services

Community
